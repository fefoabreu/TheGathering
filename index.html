Where We Are

Garopaba & Praia da Silveira

A corner of Brazil that surfers, nature lovers, and explorers have kept to themselves — until now.

🌊 Garopaba, SC

The Surf Capital

A small, authentic coastal town in Santa Catarina where world-class waves meet lush Atlantic Forest hillsides. Famed for humpback whale migrations, vibrant beach culture, and some of the freshest seafood in South Brazil.

  • World-class surf breaks for all levels
  • Humpback whale migration corridor — June to November
  • Giant sand dunes — sandboarding paradise
  • Authentic local seafood restaurants & markets
  • 2 hours from Florianópolis International Airport
🏔️ Praia da Silveira

The Hidden Gem

Praia da Silveira sits atop a dramatic hill — one of the most sought-after surf beaches in Garopaba, known for consistent swells and raw natural beauty. The Gathering sits at the very top of the Morro, with ocean views in every direction.

  • Premium surf beach — consistent year-round swells
  • Hilltop vantage with 360° ocean panoramas
  • Dense Atlantic Forest as your backdrop
  • Quiet, small community — peaceful & authentic
  • Less than 10 min from Garopaba town center
